About Our NGO

InAmigos Foundation was founded on September 23, 2020, by Mr. Govind Shukla (Founder & CEO). It is a Section 8 registered non-profit organization licensed by the Central Government and based in Chhattisgarh.

The foundation holds 80G and 12A certifications, is CSR-1 registered, NITI Aayog registered, and has IAF ISO 9001:2015 certification. These credentials reflect transparency, accountability, and high-quality service delivery.

InAmigos Foundation works across food distribution, education, women empowerment, animal welfare, environmental sustainability, and social inclusion.
